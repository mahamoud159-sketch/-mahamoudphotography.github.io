About Me

A Little
About Me

Hey, I'm Mahamoud — an Ottawa-based photographer shooting weddings, engagements, couples, and events across Ottawa, Toronto, and Montreal. I got into photography because I kept noticing that the best moments happen in the in-between — the laugh right before the first look, a quiet hand-hold during the ceremony, the way a room feels when everyone finally lets loose on the dance floor. I shoot relaxed and unobtrusive; no stiff posing, no shouting directions across a room. I just show up, get to know everyone, and let the day unfold. Being Somali-Canadian, I've grown up surrounded by big, loud, joyful celebrations, and that's shaped how I see and work — with warmth, energy, and a lot of heart. My editing leans cinematic and I get your gallery back in 7 days, which most photographers simply won't do.
