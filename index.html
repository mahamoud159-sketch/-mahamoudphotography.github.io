About Me

Your Story,
Through My Lens

Hi — I'm Mahamoud, an Ottawa-based photographer specializing in weddings, engagements, couples, events, and automotive photography across Ottawa, Toronto, and Montreal.

I got into photography because the moments that matter most deserve to be preserved with honesty and artistry. My shooting style is calm, unobtrusive, and story-driven — always looking for the authentic emotion behind the moment, not just the posed version of it.

My cinematic editing style gives every photo rich tones and a timeless look. And with my guaranteed 7-day turnaround, your photos are back in your hands before the excitement fades.

For weddings, I recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance — especially for peak season dates (May through October). Many popular weekend dates fill up quickly. For portrait sessions, baby showers, and events, 4–8 weeks notice is usually sufficient, though I occasionally have shorter-notice availability.

My style is cinematic and documentary — I focus on capturing authentic emotions and genuine moments rather than stiff, posed photos. I love rich tones, careful light, and a timeless look. Think: real laughter, stolen glances, and moments that feel exactly as they did on the day.

It depends on your package. Essential includes 100+ edited photos, Signature includes 400+, and Premium includes 600+. For full weddings, the number is often higher — the more activity happening, the more photos you receive. Every photo is individually hand-edited, not batch-processed.

I guarantee delivery within 7 days for all packages — one of the fastest turnarounds in Ottawa. Most photographers take 4–8 weeks. Premium package clients also receive a 48-hour sneak peek gallery. Your full gallery is delivered via a private, downloadable online gallery.

Rain doesn't ruin photos — it often makes them more dramatic and romantic. I always come prepared with backup plans and experience shooting in all weather conditions. Some of my most stunning shots have been in the rain. I'll guide you through everything on the day.
